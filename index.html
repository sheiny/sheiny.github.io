About

I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. degree in Computer Science with an emphasis on Computer-Aided Hardware Design. I have a strong background in machine learning, algorithms, parallel computing, data structures, and optimization problems. I really love programming and understanding how things work. For my thesis, I am developing a learning model to predict early Design Rule Violations (DRVs) using the open-source EDA tool OpenROAD and the circuits from OpenCores.
